<p>
The <b>initvar</b> program is intended to test the effects of schema congruence/incongruence.
</p>
<p>
We expect more experienced programmers to produce more errors in the <span class="version">bothbad</span> and <span class="version">onebad</span> version because they are likely to incorrectly infer intent. While less experienced programmers will likely be slower, we expect them to make fewer errors.
</p>
