<p>
This simple set of programs is intended to test the effects of vertical spacing on whether or not the final <b>print</b> statement belongs in the <b>for</b> loop.
</p>
<p>
We expect that less experienced programmers will be more likely to mistakenly attribute the final <b>print</b> statement to the main body as the vertical space increases. More experienced programmers will likely verify the indentation level and not produce the error.
</p>
